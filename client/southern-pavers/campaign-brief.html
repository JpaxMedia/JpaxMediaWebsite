Overview

The Strategy

Spring is the highest-value window of the year for Southern Pavers. This campaign stacks two proven assets into a deliberate three-stage funnel.

Key Insight
The AI Visualizer is the differentiator
No local competitor can show a homeowner their own backyard transformed before signing a contract. Traffic campaigns land at southernpaverssc.app — not a generic homepage. Every visitor who uses the tool becomes a warm retargeting audience.
Stage 1
Bigfoot Spring Video
The Bigfoot firepit campaign hit $0.028/ThruPlay with a 44.7% completion rate — nearly 3× industry average. A new spring creative builds a 10,000+ warm audience cheaply.
Stage 2
AI Visualizer Traffic
The SC Homeowners traffic campaign previously hit $0.36/LPV — 28% below industry benchmarks. Portfolio creative drives warm audiences to the interactive AI tool.
Stage 3
Retargeting — App Visitors
App visitors showed intent by interacting with the tool. The November messaging campaign hit $9.75/conversation on cold audiences. App visitors convert cheaper, at higher quality.

Three-Stage Campaign

Each stage has a defined role. Awareness builds the audience. Consideration converts them to app visitors. Conversion turns intent into conversations.

01
Awareness
Bigfoot Spring Video Campaign
Objective: Video Views Budget: $7/day Audience: Cold — SC Homeowners 35–65
Why It Works
  • Bigfoot creative hit $0.028/ThruPlay in October — 3× industry average
  • Facebook algorithm favors personality-driven content with strong completion rates
  • Spring angle adds urgency: "schedules fill fast" without saying it directly
Creative Direction
  • Bigfoot emerges from woods surveying a bare winter backyard
  • Cut to stunning finished Southern Pavers patio
  • Word-by-word captions for sound-off viewing
  • 20–25 seconds, vertical format for Reels + Feed

Launch Schedule

Four-Week Rollout

Staged launch gives each campaign time to exit the learning phase before adding complexity. Stage 3 launches when the retargeting pool is ready.

Week 1
Launch Stages 1 + 2
  • Publish Bigfoot spring video — ThruPlay optimization
  • Launch AI Visualizer traffic campaign — LPV optimization
  • Monitor learning phase Days 1–5
  • Confirm Facebook Pixel on southernpaverssc.app
Week 2
Review + Geo Rebalance
  • Compare CPV and LPV by market
  • Shift geo if >20% variance between markets
  • Assess ThruPlay rate — refresh if <35%
  • App visitor pool check — target 300+
Week 3
Launch Stage 3
  • Create Custom Audiences: app visitors + video viewers 50%+
  • Launch retargeting at $2/day
  • A/B test two Stage 3 creatives
  • Review full-funnel cost flow
Week 4
Full-Funnel Evaluation
  • Calculate cost per conversation + CPL
  • Assess lead quality by project type
  • Scale Stage 2 +20–30% if LPV holding
  • Plan April budget heading into peak season
