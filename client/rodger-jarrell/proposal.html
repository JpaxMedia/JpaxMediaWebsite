JPAX Media LLC Proposal - Rodger C. Jarrell Real Estate & Mortgages

01 Introduction

JPAX Media will create and manage a cleaner digital presence for Rodger C. Jarrell Real Estate & Mortgages, Inc. The engagement is built around three priorities: a professional website, a simple intake path for people to send their information, and consistent Facebook content that keeps the business visible.

This proposal keeps the first phase intentionally focused. The goal is to launch quickly, improve trust, and create a simple system that can be adjusted after the first 90 days.

Due now to begin $1,650

This starts the website build, account setup, and first-month management planning without requiring the full project amount all at once.

  • $950 website deposit
  • $500 first-month management deposit
  • $200 Facebook Business / Meta setup
