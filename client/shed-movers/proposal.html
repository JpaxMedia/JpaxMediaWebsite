Before / After

Before · Current site Basic information, but not much direction.

The current site lists moving services, shipping containers, contact info, and location details. It works as a brochure, but it does not strongly guide a visitor toward the right next step.

After · Redesign preview Sharper positioning, better routing, and quote-first conversion.

The redesigned site puts Kevin's local expertise up front, gives every visitor a clear path, and uses a fast quote form, inventory cards, and build-and-price starter to turn attention into conversations.

  • MessageFrom generic "moving services" to "Not just any shed. Kevin's shed lot." That makes the site feel personal, local, and easier to remember.
  • NavigationFrom a few broad pages to purpose-built paths: Shop Sheds, Build & Price, Services, Storage Guides, Contact, and Get Quote.
  • ConversionFrom "contact us today" to a visible fast quote box with service type, ZIP/city, and note fields right in the hero.
  • SalesFrom listing products to helping customers choose: shed cards, container options, recommended use cases, and a starter estimate flow.
  • TrustFrom hidden credibility to visible proof: 10+ years moving sheds, four states served, RTO container options, DOT registration, and local guidance from Kevin.
More qualified calls

Visitors pre-select what they need before reaching out, so Kevin can respond with context instead of starting cold.

Better mobile buying flow

The new layout is built around quick taps, clear actions, and phone-first browsing for customers already standing in a yard or on a lot.

Stronger local brand

The site keeps the practical catalog feel but adds personality, structure, and a polished first impression.
