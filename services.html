Engagement Models

BUILD. RISE. SCALE.

Pick the shape of the work. Most engagements settle into one of these. Larger ones combine them.

Model

BUILD

One-time project work to ship the system the business needs now.

  • Websites
  • Brand systems
  • Video
  • AI systems
  • Dashboards
  • Custom apps
50% up front. 50% at delivery.
Client owns IP at final payment.
Two revision rounds included.
30-day post-delivery support.
Timeline begins upon receipt of deposit.
Model

RISE

Monthly retainers that keep the system producing once it is built.

  • Social management
  • AI creative content

  • Ads support
  • Reporting
First month due to begin.
30-day written notice to cancel.
IP transfers monthly at payment.
Model

SCALE

Larger custom engagements combining BUILD and RISE for sustained leverage.

  • Website + content engine
  • Website + dashboard
  • Website + operating system
  • Creator operating layer
  • Local business growth system
Scoped per engagement.
Deposit + monthly structure.
Documented system handoff.
