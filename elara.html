How It Works

Three steps. Then the system runs.

Onboarding is the part where Elara learns you. After that, you stay creative — Elara handles the rest, with you in the approval seat whenever you want to be.

01
Onboard — voice training
Elara reads your existing content, scripts, captions, and brand voice. By the end of week one, the Writing Agent sounds like you on a good day.
02
Approve drafts
For the first few weeks, every output runs past you. You approve, edit, or kill. Elara learns the patterns. The approval load drops fast.
03
System runs
Content publishes. DMs get drafted. Brand deals get tracked. You check in. You make the creative calls. The operating layer disappears underneath.
