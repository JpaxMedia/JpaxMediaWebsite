What Elara Covers

Not a content calendar. An operating layer.

Creators do not need another scheduler. They need the system underneath the content: who is paying, where it is going, what is working, and what the brand actually owns.

Website ownership

  • Creator-owned site, not a rented profile
  • Brand assets and identity live in one place
  • Press, sponsor, and contact paths instrumented
  • Built for long-term equity, not platform churn

Content planning

  • Posting rhythm and campaign organization
  • Series, drops, and recurring formats tracked
  • Cross-platform structure
  • Capacity-aware planning, not aspirational

Sponsor & brand deals

  • Sponsor pipeline visibility
  • Idea routing and pitch organization
  • Delivery schedule for paid posts
  • Brand-deal status without spreadsheet chaos

Performance & revenue

  • Revenue tracked directionally, not pretend-precise
  • Reporting that reflects real momentum
  • Clear inputs for what to build next
