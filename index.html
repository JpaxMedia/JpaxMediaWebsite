Meet The Founder

Julian Johnson is the creator behind JPax Media, a creative studio built on the blend of storytelling and AI-powered technology.

Starting out in videography and editing, Julian worked with local businesses, sports programs, and entrepreneurs to capture their stories on camera. As digital marketing evolved, he leaned into AI — using it to streamline workflows, create content faster, and unlock new possibilities for social media, ads, websites, and branding.

Outside of client projects, Julian is a hands-on creative: testing new gear, building designs, exploring drone videography, and experimenting with AI storytelling. He brings that same curiosity and energy into every project, with one goal — to help people and businesses grow online in smarter, more creative ways.
