FAQ

Greenville AI website design questions, answered.

What is an AI-powered website?

An AI-powered website is a site built and run with AI in the workflow, not just a static brochure. JPAX uses AI to move faster on design and content, then wires the site into the systems behind it: lead capture, follow-up, content rhythm, and reporting. The result is a website that does work for the business, not one that just sits online.

How much does a website cost in Greenville, SC?

JPAX website projects start at $3,500 for a Local Website Foundation and scale up for larger service businesses, teams, and brand-plus-web systems. Pricing depends on scope, integrations, and whether the site connects to follow-up, dashboards, or automation. You can see entry points on the pricing page or send a project brief for a scoped quote.

How long does it take to build a new website?

Most local service-business websites move from kickoff to launch in a few weeks, depending on content readiness and how many systems are connected. Because JPAX builds with AI in the workflow, early drafts and structure come together quickly, and the time is spent on getting positioning, proof, and conversion paths right.

Do I own my website, or am I renting it?

You own it. JPAX builds owned systems, not rented templates locked inside a platform that disappears when you stop paying. The website, the content, and the structure belong to your business.

Can my website connect to lead follow-up and reporting?

Yes. That is the point. JPAX connects the website to intake, follow-up queues, content rhythm, and reporting so leads do not get lost between forms, inboxes, and memory. AI can draft follow-ups and summaries, but changes that touch real business data stay behind human approval.
