Solar care

Pollen season can dull solar panels.

Dust, pollen, leaves, and bird debris can sit on solar panels and limit how cleanly sunlight reaches the surface. Regular exterior care helps homeowners protect the investment they already made.

Gentle cleaning is the point.

Solar panel washing should be framed around careful access, non-abrasive surface care, and a clear understanding of the roof and panel layout before work begins.

Requests should capture the right details.

Panel count, roof access, visible buildup, tree cover, and whether performance has recently dropped are all useful details before scheduling.
