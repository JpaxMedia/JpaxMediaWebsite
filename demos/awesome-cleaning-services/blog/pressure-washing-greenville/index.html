Pressure washing

When should you pressure wash in Greenville?

In the Upstate, pollen, humidity, shaded siding, and summer storms can make exterior surfaces look tired quickly. A professional pressure washing visit can help restore curb appeal and make walkways, driveways, patios, and entryways feel cared for again.

Spring and early summer are strong windows.

After pollen season, many properties show buildup on siding, concrete, glass, steps, and outdoor living spaces. Cleaning during this window helps reset the property before heavier summer use.

High-traffic surfaces may need attention sooner.

Driveways, sidewalks, storefront entries, pool decks, and patios can collect dirt and organic growth faster than low-traffic areas. If the surface looks dark, slippery, or uneven, it may be time to schedule service.
